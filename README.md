**LibLink is an intelligent library management companion designed for students and readers.**
It helps you:

- Never miss due dates with deadline tracking and reminders

- Check availability of books in your library in real time

- Get personalized book recommendations powered by AI

- Quickly search, filter, and explore books by genre, author, tags, and more

- Whether you're trying to finish assignments before the due date or want to explore new genres, LibLink keeps your reading organized and smart.
